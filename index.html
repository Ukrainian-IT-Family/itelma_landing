About company

The developer is known for developing, designing and building commercial real estate under the order. In the briefcase Itelma construction of several industrial parks, class A business center, reconstruction of the former buildings of the ZIL plant, research and laboratory and industrial premises.

Itelma belongs to the group Itelma companies. Beyond the developer the GC includes organizations involved in aircraft construction and electronics. The number of employees exceeds 3000 people.

New project ITELMA - these are two modern 29-storey residential towers with a building area of 1.26 hectares. The residential complex called Newton Apartments is located next to the ITELMA technopark, built developer in 2015.

The developer has established itself in the market as a reliable developer planning the construction process in detail. ITELMA DEVELOPMENT carefully thinks over layouts and infrastructure, and also pays great attention to technological solutions and innovation.
